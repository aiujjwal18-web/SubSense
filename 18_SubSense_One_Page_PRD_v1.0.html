Problem Statement & Objective

The Why

Current Problem

Modern users pay for recurring subscriptions across entertainment, productivity, AI tools, education, cloud services, fitness, and memberships. Because these payments renew automatically, awareness breaks down long before the decision ever gets made.

Awareness gaps
  • Users forget renewal dates
  • Users underestimate annual spending
  • Duplicate or overlapping services go unnoticed
Decision gaps
  • Shared subscription payments become difficult to track
  • Cancellation decisions are delayed until after renewal
  • Existing tools mostly report spending after the fact

Vision

Build the most trusted AI-assisted subscription decision platform that helps users understand recurring spending, stay informed before renewals, and make confident subscription decisions without taking control away from them.

Mission

Help users become aware of recurring subscription spending through timely reminders, financial context, and AI-assisted decision support — while ensuring users always remain in control of renewal, cancellation, sharing, and payment decisions.

Guardrails

The How We Stay Honest

Key risks

  • AI overreach — mitigated by enforcing BR-001 in backend and frontend copy
  • RLS mistakes — mitigated by testing owner/member/admin access paths
  • Scope expansion — mitigated by MVP scope discipline and change control
  • Defects found too late — mitigated by the Phase 9.5 mid-build stress checkpoint (Week 3)
  • Payment confusion — mitigated by labeling Razorpay as Test Mode everywhere

Explicitly out of scope (v1)

  • Automatic bank account or email scanning
  • Apple/Google subscription sync
  • Automated third-party cancellation execution
  • Production billing / live Razorpay payments
  • Browser extension, mobile app, enterprise administration

Dependencies & assumptions

  • Users are willing to manually enter subscriptions without bank/email auto-import Assumption
  • AI model can produce high-confidence renewal insight from manually entered data alone Assumption
  • Email-first reminders (no push/mobile app) are sufficient for MVP engagement Assumption
  • Razorpay Test Mode is an acceptable substitute for live payment validation in this capstone context Assumption
